The Problem

Selling on multiple platforms is a second full-time job — and it's getting worse

  • The average successful seller operates on 3+ platforms: Shopify, Etsy, eBay, Amazon, Faire
  • Every platform has a separate dashboard, separate rules, and a separate workflow
  • A price change on 200 products across 3 platforms means hours of manual, repetitive work
  • Inventory errors and inconsistent listings are nearly unavoidable at scale
  • Enterprise tools (Linnworks, Sellbrite, ChannelAdvisor) start at $500–$1,500/month — completely out of reach for independent sellers
  • No tool on the market lets a seller simply describe what they want done and have it happen
  • Independent sellers are spending more time managing platforms than running their business

Product

Four core capabilities — live today at tandril.org

⚡ AI Commands

Natural language bulk actions across all connected platforms. Price updates, inventory changes, listing edits — reviewed before execution, undoable after. No coding, no imports, no spreadsheets.

🧠 Orion AI Advisor

Proactive business intelligence built in. Orion monitors your stores, surfaces low stock alerts, identifies growth opportunities, and delivers strategic recommendations in real time.

📦 Unified Inventory

One view across every connected platform. Track stock levels, filter by status, and act on low-stock items before they become lost sales — without jumping between dashboards.

🔄 Automated Workflows

Schedule recurring tasks — inventory email reports, price guardrails, SEO fixes — that run automatically. Set it once, let Tandril handle the rest.
